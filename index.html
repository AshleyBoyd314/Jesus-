Jesus Messiah

The Son of God who came to take away the sin of the world

Jesus' life as recorded in the Holy Bible:

  • Mary was told of her son Jesus' birth by the angel Gabriel that she would conceive through the power of the Holy Spirit despite her having never been with a man herself.
  • Jesus' mother Mery gave birth to Him in a manger because there was no room for them in the inn.
  • When Jesus was 30 years old God the Father confirmed him as his beloved son who he was well-pleased in. This happened after Jesus' baptism by John the baptizer.
  • Jesus started his ministry at 30 years old
  • Jesus healed the sick, cast out demons, raised the dead, and did signs and miracles to show he was the Son of God.
  • Jesus was betrayed by one of his disciples and was handed over to the authorities to be crucified.
  • At the age of 33 years old Jesus was crucified. He died and rosed again on the third day that whoever believe on him will have eternal life!

"And this is eternal life, that they know you, the only true God, and Jesus Christ whom you have sent."

(John 17:3, ESV)
